Flame detection:

Detection of smode using a smoke gas sensor with a well determined threshold.

About Us

We propose a fire detection system to minimize the losses due to the fires, which cause material losses and damage the human life. It is a progressive web application connected to total iot system and devices installed perfectly inside a particular space.
